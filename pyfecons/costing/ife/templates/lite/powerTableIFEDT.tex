\section{Power Accounting Table}

\textbf{Description:} The Power Accounting Table provides a comprehensive breakdown of power flows within the inertial fusion energy (IFE) system. It tracks power from the fusion reaction through various conversion and recirculation processes to the final net electric power output. The table is organized into three main categories: (1) Output power from the fusion reaction, including fusion power, alpha power, neutron power, and their conversion to thermal and electric power; (2) Recirculating power required to operate the plant systems, including power for target factory, implosion and ignition lasers, cooling systems, auxiliary systems, and other plant operations; and (3) Final output metrics including scientific Q (fusion power to input power ratio), engineering Q, recirculating power fraction, and net electric power available for the grid. This table is essential for understanding the energy balance and efficiency of the IFE power plant design.
